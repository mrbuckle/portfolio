He's Finally Arrived.  Kevin Buckle.UX/UI Designed.

Hello, I am Kevin Buckle. A Ux/UI Visual Designer. I have a background in fine art, graphic design/ illustration, and cultural philosophy.

Not only am I a dedicated follower of fashion, I am passionate about creativity and developing impactful design solutions. I enjoy problem solving, in-depth analysis, and wrestling with fine details.

Blessed having worked in graphic design creating logos, apparel and merchandise, I leverage this and my background in critical thinking to enhance my experience as a UX/UI designer to develop strong modern designs and interfaces for users. I love design and the journey it takes me on.
